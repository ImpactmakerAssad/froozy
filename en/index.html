Our story

Fruit deserves the spotlight

We turn real fruit into a snack that crackles. Nothing added, nothing taken away — except the water.

Most snacks promise everything and deliver little. We flipped it: a bag with exactly two things inside — fruit and fruit. No added sugar, no concentrate, no fine print.

Pull the water out and fruit suddenly turns light, intense and crunchy. We call it crrrunch. An apple becomes a crisp, a strawberry becomes a crackle — and nothing gets lost along the way.

From lunchboxes to breakfast buffets: Froozy fits anywhere people deserve something tasty that happens to be smart too. Snack the bright side.

Fruithasalwaysbeenperfect.Wejustmakeitirresistible.

How it works

One cool trick

01

Fresh fruit

Two kinds of fruit per bag. Which also happens to be the entire ingredient list.

02

Frozen to −80 °C

Colder than a polar night. Every drop of flavour freezes right where it lives.

03

Vacuum pulls the water out

The ice lifts straight out as vapour. Water was the boring ingredient anyway.

04

Everything that matters stays: flavour · crunch · power

No heat, so nothing gets lost. Only one thing gets added — that crrrunch.

That's the whole story. No added sugar, no nonsense. Real fruit, real crunch, good for you.
